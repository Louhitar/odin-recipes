Lentil Pasta Bolognese

Description

An easy to make pasta bolognese recipe I use that features lentils in a delicious sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 box of lentils
  • 1-2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 1 box of tomato puree
  • 1 vegetable or beef buillon
  • Fresh parmesan
  • Salt and pepper
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Put water to boil and add pasta once boiling
  2. Dice onion and crush garlic
  3. Heat a frying pan on medium heat with oil
  4. Add onion to pan and fry until see-through
  5. Rinse and add the lentils, then the garlic and buillon
  6. Add tomato puree
  7. Add pepper to taste
  8. Lower the heat and let ingredients simmer for 5-10min
  9. Taste and add spices to your liking
  10. Add parmesan and basil to taste
  11. Strain pasta once ready and mix with sauce
  12. Serve!
