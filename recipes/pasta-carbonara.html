Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 4 strips of streaky bacon
  • 1 brown onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Mushrooms
  • 2.5dl cream
  • 1 egg
  • Fresh parmesan
  • Pepper
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Put water to boil
  2. Dice bacon and onion, crush garlic, slice mushrooms
  3. Mix cream, egg, parmesan and some pepper into a seperate bowl
  4. Fry bacon in a pan on medium heat, moving it to a plate when done
  5. Fry mushrooms and move to plate
  6. Cook pasta once water is boiling
  7. Fry onion until done, then add plate contents and garlic then stir
  8. Add sauce from bowl
  9. Strain and add pasta to pan, stirring until sauce is ready
  10. Serve
