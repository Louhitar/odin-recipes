Feta Pasta

Ingredients

  • Feta
  • Grape or Cherry tomatoes
  • Chilli flakes, chilli powder, or jalapenos
  • Pasta
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200C or 180C fan-forced
  2. Add olive oil to dish
  3. Place feta block and tomatoes in dish
  4. Cover tomatoes and feta in oil
  5. Add chilli of choice
  6. Place in oven for 25 minutes
  7. Cook pasta
  8. Crush or squash tomatoes after removing from oven
  9. Mix thoroughly and serve
